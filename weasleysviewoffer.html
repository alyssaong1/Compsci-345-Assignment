Team News and Updates

28/06/2015

Team alumni gets scouted

Just yesterday, Ron Weasley, our star player of 2014 received a call from a talent scout to represent UK in the International Quidditch World Championships held in Japan in October this year. Ron said he was ecstatic to be given the opportunity to be playing at a world class level and would like to thank everyone who helped him make it this far. Congratulations Ron!

14/06/2015

Arthur Weasley is set to play as a Legend

The revered Godfather and Founder of Weasley's Army is set to play in the Legends team this year after 10 years of retirement to date. Arthur, who says he has missed Quidditch, says he has still been playing regularly after retirement and is excited to show the world his newfound skills.
